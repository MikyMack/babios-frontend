About Babios

Clothes That Grow
With Your Child

Babios was born in 2018 when a colour-loving parent — frustrated by stiff, scratchy baby clothes — decided to do something better. Backed by expert colour science, we create clothing that is incredibly soft, responsibly made, and joyfully colourful for every child.

  • Premium GOTS-certified organic cotton — safe, breathable, and gentle on delicate skin
  • Pantone-inspired colour palettes that spark imagination and creativity
  • Sustainably made with eco-friendly packaging and responsible production
