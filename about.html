Why Choose Babios

What Makes Us Different

We obsess over every detail — from thread count to colour accuracy — so you never have to.

Why is organic cotton worth it for babies?
Newborn skin is up to 5× more permeable than adult skin. Conventional cotton carries pesticide residues that can irritate or absorb into delicate skin. GOTS-certified organic cotton eliminates this risk entirely — and feels noticeably softer too.
How does Pantone influence the collections?
Pantone's colour intelligence team collaborates with our designers each season to select palettes that are both on-trend and developmentally stimulating. High-contrast and saturated hues actively support visual development in infants.
Do Babios garments hold their colour after washing?
Absolutely. We use Pantone-certified reactive dyes — a low-impact process where dye bonds permanently with the cotton fibre at a molecular level. Colours retain their vibrancy even after 60+ washes, tested per ISO 105 standards.
Is Babios sizing inclusive for all body types?
Yes. Our fits feature generous cut grading and adjustable waistbands so children of all shapes wear comfortably. We offer sizes from 0–3 months up to age 10, with a detailed size guide on every product page.
