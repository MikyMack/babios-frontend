Keep Them Like New

Simple Care,
Lasting Softness

Follow these easy care guidelines to keep your Babios clothing as soft and vibrant as the day you first opened the pack.

🌡️
Gentle Machine Wash

Wash at 30°C on a gentle cycle. Cold water preserves the color and softness of organic cotton fibers.

🧴
Mild Detergent Only

Use a baby-safe, fragrance-free detergent. Avoid harsh chemicals, bleach, or fabric softeners with synthetic additives.

🌬️
Air Dry When Possible

Lay flat or hang to air dry in shade. This maintains the shape and extends the life of the garment naturally.

♨️
Low Heat Tumble Dry

If using a dryer, choose the lowest heat setting. High heat can shrink organic cotton and reduce its softness.

👕
Iron on Low Heat

If ironing is needed, use a cool iron and iron inside out. Never iron directly over prints or embellishments.

🚫
No Dry Cleaning

Dry cleaning chemicals can damage organic cotton and leave residues that irritate baby skin.
